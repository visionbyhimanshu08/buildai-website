FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Do I need a technical background to work with you?+
Not at all. We work with founders, business owners, and product managers at every technical level. We translate your vision into technical decisions — you stay focused on the business.
What if I only have an idea and no product yet?+
That's where we shine most. Start with a free discovery call. We'll help you validate the idea, define the MVP scope, and give you a clear plan before any money changes hands.
How is your AI development different from a regular agency?+
Traditional agencies write features. We design intelligence. Every architecture decision we make considers AI as a core system — not a plugin. Your product doesn't just do things; it learns and improves over time.
Do I own the code and intellectual property?+
Yes, 100%. Upon project completion and final payment, all source code, intellectual property, and assets are transferred to you with no ongoing licensing fees or lock-in.
What technologies do you typically use?+
We're technology-agnostic and pick the best stack for your use case. Common choices: React/Next.js for frontend, Node.js or Python for backend, PostgreSQL or MongoDB for data, and OpenAI or Anthropic models for AI.
